Support

Need help?

Contact us for StudyDuck questions, feedback, bug reports, account requests, or privacy-related requests.

Email

Reach us at studyduckservices@gmail.com.

Response time

We usually respond to support requests within 1-2 business days.

Details to include

To help us solve the issue faster, please include your device model, operating system version, StudyDuck app version, a short description, and screenshots when available.

Account and data requests

For account deletion, data access, or privacy requests, please contact us from the email address linked to your StudyDuck account.
