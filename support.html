Support

How can we help?

Send feedback, report an issue, or ask about your StudyDuck account. We usually reply within 1-2 business days.

Email

Contact the StudyDuck team at studyduckservices@gmail.com.

Helpful details to include

Please include your device type, app version, a short description of the issue, and screenshots when they help explain what happened.

Account and data requests

For account deletion or data questions, email us from the address linked to your StudyDuck account.
